Above & Bellow

2019

“Above and Bellow” is inspired by the way plants communicate and interconnect.


Creating a range of communication networks both above and below ground, plants interact with other organisms, as well as each other, ensuring their mutual survival.

Reflecting this concept, my work is formed of Silver and Titanium elements that have been layered together to form an unbreakable circle of communication. Sound wave data of bees during pollination has also been subtly woven into my work.

Focusing on traditional methods, my work aims to capture the intricacy and elegance of nature in a contemporary style.
